About Me

My name is Andrea Tai and I am originally from Taipei, Taiwan but have relocated to Santa Clara, CA. I recently graduated from Santa Clara University in June 2020 with a Communication major, Economics minor, and Management Information Systems minor. As an individual, I am hardworking to get the job done with detail and focus, which is developed through the numerous responsibilities in event planning and other activities. From the different activities, I have developed the skill to be a team player with desire to hear the opinions of others, especially when I was responsible for leading others in various organizations. As someone who has lived abroad and has come in contact with many diverse individuals, I have learned languages such as English, Spanish, Mandarin, and Taiwanese.
